About Us

With over 30 years experience in the construction industry Strucco Construction & Civil has enormous expertise and experience in the following fields:

– Building construction and maintenance
– Civil construction
– Civil roads
– Design and construct (Turnkey Projects)
– Routine roads maintenance
– Backup power systems installation and maintenance

Our aim is to continue delivering service that is beyond expectation and to be the preferred construction partner for all stakeholders while fostering the principles of equal opportunities to all.

Overview

Over the years, the owner has undertaken many challenging projects and accumulated skills, know-how and expierences in design and build solutions, project management services, building trades and related engineering works.

Today, Strucco Construction & Civil takes on the role of main contractor for small to medium size projects and performs project management services to coordinates specialist trades for Industrial/Commercial projects
